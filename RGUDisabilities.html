At RGU we take pride in ensuring that everyone is offered support to allow them to achieve their best

You can contact us at any time before or during your studies and we are happy to speak to applicants, enquirers, parents teachers and those from support organisations

We can provide:

  • Exam arrangements such as extra time
  • Dyslexia screening and evaluation
  • In-house assessment of needs
  • Application for disabled students' allowance
  • Liaison with external agencies, for example the Autism Initiatives or the RNIB
  • Support in organising in-class support
